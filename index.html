About us

Hudson furnishing is a one-stop destination for all home furniture needs, specializing in various categories such as bedroom, sofa, mattress, and kitchen furniture, with a focus on interior design. They are the exclusive Palliser custom order studio, offering over 300 fabrics or leathers for customization. The store aims to simplify the shopping experience by providing easy navigation through showrooms, showcasing designer furniture crafted with passion and attention to detail. The goal is to alleviate the stress associated with the abundance of furniture choices, offering a pleasant shopping experience. The shop intends to inspire home décor ideas and help customers turn their houses into comfortable homes.
